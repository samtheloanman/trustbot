2. Durability Clause

This Power of Attorney shall not be affected by subsequent incapacity of the principal. This Power of Attorney is durable pursuant to California Probate Code § 4124 and shall remain in full force and effect regardless of any future incapacity of the principal after the time of signing.

4. Agent Obligations

The Agent shall act in good faith and in a fiduciary capacity for my benefit and shall not commingle the principal's funds with personal funds. The Agent shall keep accurate records of all transactions taken pursuant to this Power of Attorney.

5. Reliance by Third Parties

Third parties may rely on a copy of this Power of Attorney. No person who acts in good faith in reliance on this Power of Attorney shall be liable to me or my estate. The Agent shall indemnify third parties acting in good faith in reliance upon this Power of Attorney.
