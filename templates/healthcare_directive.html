(Living Will and Healthcare Power of Attorney Combined)
Pursuant to California Probate Code § 4701
Notice to Person Completing This Form: This form lets you name an individual, called an agent, to make health care decisions for you if you lose the capacity to make your own decisions or if you want someone else to make those decisions for you now even though you have that capacity. You may also use this form to give specific instructions about any aspect of your health care. Carefully read all information before signing. Consult a lawyer if desired.
