PURPOSE OF THIS DOCUMENT: This Pour-Over Will is a companion document to the {{trust_name}}. Its primary purpose is to ensure that any assets not transferred to the trust during your lifetime are "poured over" into the trust upon your death, so that all assets are administered under a single, consistent plan. Without this Will, assets outside the trust may be subject to California intestate succession rather than your stated wishes.

I, {{grantor_name}}, of {{grantor_city}}, {{grantor_state}}, declare this to be my Last Will and Testament (Pour-Over Will), revoking all prior wills and codicils previously made by me.

Article I — Identification

  1. I am not married at the time of this Will's execution. {{#if is_married}}Correction: I am married to {{spouse_name}}.{{/if}}
  2. I make this Will freely and voluntarily, being of legal age and sound mind.

Article II — Debts and Expenses

  1. I direct my Personal Representative to pay all my legally enforceable debts, funeral expenses, and costs of administration of my estate from the assets of my estate as soon as practicable after my death, subject to any right I may have to direct otherwise.

Article III — Pour-Over Bequest

  1. I give, devise, and bequeath all of my property, real, personal, and mixed, of whatever nature and wherever situated, that I own at my death and that has not been transferred to the {{trust_name}} (the "Living Trust") during my lifetime, to the then-acting Trustee of the Living Trust to be held, administered, and distributed in accordance with the provisions of that trust, including any amendments made prior to my death.
  2. If the Living Trust has been revoked before my death or if no trust exists at the time of my death, I direct that my estate be distributed to {{#each beneficiaries}}{{this.name}} ({{this.share}}%){{#unless @last}}, {{/unless}}{{/each}}, in the proportions indicated.
  3. The Living Trust need not be in existence at the time I execute this Will. This Will incorporates by reference the terms of the Living Trust as they exist at my death, pursuant to California Probate Code § 6300.

Article IV — Personal Representative

  1. I appoint {{successor_trustee_list}}, in the order listed, as Personal Representative(s) of this Will. No bond shall be required of any Personal Representative.
  2. My Personal Representative shall have all powers granted to personal representatives under California law, including the Independent Administration of Estates Act (Cal. Prob. Code §§ 10400–10592), without the need for court approval for ordinary estate transactions.

Article V — Guardian for Minor Children (if applicable)

  1. If I have minor children at the time of my death, I nominate {{successor_trustee_list}} as guardian(s) of the person and estate of any such minor children, in the order listed. No bond shall be required of any guardian.

Article VI — No-Contest Clause

  1. If any beneficiary under this Will contests or attacks this Will or any of its provisions, any share or interest in my estate given to the contesting beneficiary under this Will is revoked, unless the contest is brought in good faith and upon probable cause (Cal. Prob. Code § 21311).

Article VII — Governing Law

  1. This Will shall be governed, construed, and administered in accordance with the laws of the State of {{execution_state}}.
